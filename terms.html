1. Program Description

HabitGuard (habitguard.ai) provides AI-driven maintenance dispatch tools for property managers. Our SMS-based service allows tenants and property managers to communicate regarding maintenance requests, Tier-1 troubleshooting, vendor scheduling, and demo coordination.

By using our service or submitting your phone number via our website, you agree to these Terms of Service.

2. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on user interaction. You can expect to receive messages when you initiate a maintenance request, when a status update occurs, when our AI agent requires more information to triage your issue, or when coordinating a product demo. All interactions are logged with timestamps to support compliance record generation for property managers.

4. Support & Opt-Out
