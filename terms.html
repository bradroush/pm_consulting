1. Program Description

Brad Roush Systems provides AI-driven operational tools, including an SMS-based maintenance triage assistant. This service allows tenants and property managers to communicate regarding maintenance requests, troubleshooting, and vendor scheduling.

2. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on user interaction. You can expect to receive messages when you initiate a maintenance request, when a status update occurs, or when our AI agent requires more information to triage your issue.

3. Cost

Message and data rates may apply for any messages sent to you from us and to us from you. If you have any questions about your text plan or data plan, it is best to contact your wireless provider.

4. Support & Opt-Out

  • STOP: You can cancel the SMS service at any time. Just text "STOP" to our number. After you send the SMS message "STOP" to us, we will send you an SMS message to confirm that you have been unsubscribed. After this, you will no longer receive SMS messages from us.
  • HELP: If you are experiencing issues with the messaging program you can reply with the keyword "HELP" for more assistance, or you can get help directly at [Your Email Address].
