1. Business Identity

HabitGuard (habitguard.ai) is an AI-powered property maintenance dispatch service. For questions about this policy, contact us at brad@habitguard.ai.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including your name, email address, and phone number when you request a demo or interact with our maintenance triage systems.

We may also collect usage data such as the content of SMS messages sent to or from a HabitGuard number as part of the maintenance coordination service, solely for the purpose of providing and improving that service. All maintenance-related interactions are logged with timestamps to generate compliance records in accordance with California Civil Code § 1941.1 habitability requirements.

3. SMS Privacy & Data Sharing (Carrier Compliance)

Mobile Information Sharing: No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. All other categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

We do not sell, rent, or lease our customer lists to third parties.

HabitGuard's SMS program is used solely for maintenance coordination, demo scheduling, and service notifications. It is not used for marketing or promotional messaging.

4. SMS Consent & Opt-Out

By providing your phone number and checking the consent box on our demo request form, you consent to receive SMS messages from HabitGuard for the purpose of maintenance coordination, demo scheduling, or service updates. Consent is obtained via web form opt-in or direct keyword interaction. For tenants of property management clients, consent is obtained when the tenant initiates contact by texting a HabitGuard-assigned number provided to them by their property manager via lease agreement or onboarding materials.

  • Opt-Out: You may revoke consent at any time by replying STOP to any message. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent.
  • Help: You may reply HELP for more information or contact us directly at brad@habitguard.ai.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Sensitive operational data transmitted through our AI agents is encrypted in transit and stored securely. We do not retain SMS message content beyond what is necessary to provide the maintenance coordination service.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our services after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
