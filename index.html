How It Works

Three steps. No app downloads. No portals. Just SMS — the channel your tenants already use.

Your tenants send maintenance requests via SMS to a dedicated HabitGuard number. No app, no login required.

2

AI Classifies and Responds Within Seconds

The AI agent determines urgency, provides Tier-1 troubleshooting for routine issues, and asks clarifying questions when needed. Every interaction is logged.

Emergencies trigger immediate alerts to you and your vendors. Routine issues are resolved or queued — with a full compliance record ready if you ever need it.
